Privacy Policy / Terms

Last updated: 2026-02-03

1) What data we collect

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Email address
  • Date of birth
  • Consent confirmation (that you accepted this policy)
  • Registration time (when you signed up)

2) Why we collect it (purpose)

We collect this data to manage event registrations and contact attendees with event-related information (for example updates or changes). Date of birth may be used to verify age requirements for the event.

3) Legal basis

Your data is processed based on your consent, given when you tick the checkbox and submit the registration form.

4) Storage and security

Your data is stored securely and is accessible only to authorized administrators. We apply access controls and technical measures to reduce risks such as unauthorized access and data leakage.

5) Retention and deletion

We keep your data until the event is completed and then delete it within 14 days. You can request deletion at any time.

6) Your rights

  • Access: request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: request to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: request deletion of your data.

7) Contact

To exercise your rights, contact the event organizer (teacher/school).
